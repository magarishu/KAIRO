# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Vision
KAIRO is an institutional-grade cloud trade copier designed for prop firm traders, fund managers, and retail traders.

## Goals
- Provide sub-millisecond trade replication across multiple broker accounts.
- Enforce strict risk parameters to protect trader capital.
- Deliver real-time analytics and telemetry.

## Core Features
1. **Multi-Account Trade Replication**: Sync trades instantly across unlimited MetaTrader 4/5 accounts.
2. **Institutional Risk Engine**: Daily loss limits, weekly profit targets, and trailing drawdown protection.
3. **Automated Billing**: SaaS subscription management via Razorpay.
4. **Secure Infrastructure**: Protected credentials, MetaApi integration, and isolated environment variables.
